Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Connect?

Digital Connect is an internet café management system that allows users to access computers, internet services, and educational resources.

How do I create an account?

Click on the Register page, complete the registration form, and submit your details.

How do I reset my password?

Open the Forgot Password page and follow the instructions to create a new password.

Can I book a computer online?

Yes. Registered users can reserve computers and internet services through the booking system.

Who can use Digital Connect?

Students, professionals, and community members can use the platform.
